<center>
<h1>React Project Folder Structures</h1>
</center>

This is just a reference for some ways you can structure your react project



---
#### Level 1 Grouping by file type
---

- Small to Medium projects
- simple
- Disadvantages: 
    -   Will inflate quickly 
    -   No seperation of business concerns

```text
└── src/
    ├── assets/
    ├── api/
    ├── configs/
    ├── components/
    │   ├── SignUpForm.tsx
    │   ├── Employees.tsx
    │   ├── PaymentForm.tsx
    │   └── Button.tsx
    ├── hooks/
    │   ├── usePayment.ts
    │   ├── useUpdateEmployee.ts
    │   ├── useEmployees.ts
    │   └── useAuth.tsx
    ├── lib/
    ├── services/
    ├── states/
    └── utils/
```


---
#### level 2: Grouping by "File Types"  and Features
---

- Medium to large
- Simple
- Disadvantages
  - Logic spread across multiple folders

```text 
└── src/
    ├── assets/
    ├── api/
    ├── configs/
    ├── components/
    │   ├── auth/
    │   │   └── SignUpForm.tsx
    │   ├── payment/
    │   │   └── PaymentForm.tsx
    │   ├── common/
    │   │   └── Button.tsx
    │   └── employees/
    │       ├── EmployeeList.tsx
    │       └── EmployeeSummary.tsx
    ├── hooks/
    │   ├── auth/
    │   │   └── useAuth.ts
    │   ├── payment/
    │   │   └── usePayment.ts
    │   └── employees/
    │       ├── useEmployees.ts
    │       └── useUpdateEmployee.ts
    ├── lib/
    ├── services/
    ├── states/
    └── utils/
```


---
#### Level 3: Grouping by Features/Modules
---
- Large to Complex
- Stuff is clearly grouped
- Features/Modules are clear representation real world objects
- Disadvantages:
  - Have to be well aware of business logic to make the right grouping

```text
└── src/
    ├── assets/
    ├── modules/
    |   ├── core/
    │   │   ├── components/
    │   │   ├── design-system/
    │   │   │   └── Button.tsx
    │   │   ├── hooks/
    │   │   ├── lib/
    │   │   └── utils/
    │   ├── payment/
    │   │   ├── components/
    │   │   │   └── PaymentForm.tsx
    │   │   ├── hooks/
    │   │   │   └── usePayment.ts
    │   │   ├── lib/
    │   │   ├── services/
    │   │   ├── states/
    │   │   └── utils/
    │   ├── auth/
    │   │   ├── components/
    │   │   │   └── SignUpForm.tsx
    │   │   ├── hooks/
    │   │   │   └── useAuth.ts
    │   │   ├── lib/
    │   │   ├── services/
    │   │   ├── states/
    │   │   └── utils/
    │   └── employees/
    │       ├── components/
    │       │   ├── EmployeeList.tsx
    │       │   └── EmployeeSummary.tsx
    │       ├── hooks/
    │       │   ├── useEmployees.ts
    │       │   └── useUpdateEmployee.ts
    │       ├── services/
    │       ├── states/
    │       └── utils/
    └── ...
```
